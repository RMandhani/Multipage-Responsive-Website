Physical Fitness

What is a diet plan ?

The diet plan acts as a bespoke template to steer your eating behaviour, exercise and lifestyle management towards optimal health and wellbeing.

What is a calorie surplus plan ?

A caloric surplus is when the number of calories that we consume is higher than the number of calories we burn. In a caloric surplus, our body mass increases.

What is a calorie deficit plan ?

In simple terms, burning more calories than you consume is referred to as a calorie-deficit diet.

What foods can I consume if I dont want to follow a plan ?

Daily lifestyle Indian food i.e., dal-chawal, roti-sabzi, etc. and you can also eat fast food once a week if you are going to gym reguarly, or else once a month.
